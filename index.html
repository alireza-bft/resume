About

Graduate researcher across “Civil & Water Engineering”, “Soil–Water–Ecosystem Sciences”, and “Ocean Engineering”, driving advances in hydrological and hydrodynamic modeling, remote sensing, and AI for environmental applications. Over 7 years expertise in integrating deep learning, VIS/NIR and active/passive MW sensing, GIS programming, and physics-based models into streamlined workflows for monitoring key hydrological processes, including compound flooding, soil moisture, drought, evapotranspiration, crop-water stress, and streamflow.
